ABOUT DASHMESH GROUP

Established in the year 2000, we, Dashmesh Overseas India, are one of the renowned manufacturers and exporters of a wide range of blister packing material, vacuum forming & thermoforming packaging material. These are appreciated for high end features such as light weight, recyclable, resistance against varied temperature conditions, and durability. These are meeting the requirements of various packaging needs of diverse industries.

Quality being our forte, we leave no stone unturned in maintaining it in our range of packaging material. We check our range on well defined parameters such as toxic, raw material used, and tamper proof, so as to offer a defect free range at clients’ end. Our experts also ensure that clients' specific requirements are met in terms of color and design so as to attain maximum client satisfaction. Further, owing to our ethical business practices and vast distribution network, we have been able to cater our range to clients based all across African and Gulf countries.

Led under the dynamic leadership of our visionary & intellectual entrepreneur Mr. Balbir Singh, we have been able to launch ourselves as a prominent business organization. With his sharp insight, our company has been able to reach the heights of success.
